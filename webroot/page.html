How to Play
Objective:
Find the mystery word by digging around the island. Be the top pirate each day by solving the word faster than any other scumbags.

Moving around the Board:
Select the cell where you want to move to. You can move up, down, left, and right by clicking around the map. Find the shortest way to your desired location.

Energy:
Each move costs 1 unit of energy. Removing obstacles costs units of energy. Removing rocks costs 2 units of energy. You can find energy boosts on the map. Running out of energy will end the game.
